隱私政策 · Privacy Policy

最後更新 Last updated: 2026-05-21

1. 概覽 Overview

本政策說明 MediaGrab 網站（本下載頁）與 MediaGrab 桌面軟體如何處理你的資料。 MediaGrab 是免費的個人影音備份工具。

This policy explains how the MediaGrab website (this download page) and the MediaGrab desktop software handle your data. MediaGrab is a free personal video backup tool.

2. 桌面軟體 Desktop Software

MediaGrab 桌面軟體在你自己的電腦本機運行。它不會收集、儲存或上傳你的 下載紀錄、檔案內容、瀏覽行為或任何個人識別資料到我們或任何第三方伺服器。所有下載的檔案 只儲存在你自己的電腦。

The MediaGrab desktop software runs entirely locally on your own computer. It does not collect, store, or transmit your download history, file contents, browsing behavior, or any personally identifiable information to us or any third party. All downloaded files stay on your own machine.

3. 廣告與第三方 Advertising & Third Parties

  • 放置 cookie 或使用類似技術
  • 收集你的 IP 位址、裝置與瀏覽器資訊以投放廣告
  • 依其各自的隱私政策運作

我們不控制這些第三方的資料處理方式。請參閱各廣告商的隱私政策： Monetag · Adsterra。

This download page and optional in-app sponsor links may be served by third-party ad networks (e.g. Monetag, Adsterra). These services may place cookies, collect your IP address, device, and browser information to serve ads, and operate under their own privacy policies. We do not control how these third parties process data.

4. 關閉廣告 Opting Out of Ads

Within the MediaGrab desktop software, you can disable all ads and sponsor links at any time via Settings → "Disable Ads".
